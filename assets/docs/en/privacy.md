# Privacy Policy

## 1. Information We Collect
Since Bench Breakthrough is designed with user privacy in mind, the Developer collects minimal data.
- **Workout Data**: Stored locally on your device.
- **Diagnostics**: Stored locally on your device.

## 2. In-App Purchases
Transaction information is handled securely by store providers (Apple App Store / Google Play Store). The Developer does not store your credit card information.

## 3. Data Usage
The Developer uses collected data solely to:
- Provide and improve Bench Breakthrough services.
- Process transactions.
